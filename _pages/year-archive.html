Overarching guideline: The Feynman method of learning

  1. Choose a concept and study it.
  2. Teach it to someone, like a child, who is unfamiliar with the topic.
  3. Revise to fill in the gaps.
  4. Review and simplify.

Repeat steps 2 and 4 until you can explain the concept in your own words.
